ChatGPT Checkout Link Generator (India)

Drag the button below to your bookmarks bar:

📌 ChatGPT IN Checkout

How to use

  1. Drag the green button above to your bookmarks bar
  2. Open chatgpt.com and sign in with the target account
  3. Click the bookmark while on chatgpt.com
  4. Choose the plan in the popup (1 = Plus, 2 = Go)
  5. Copy the generated checkout link from the next popup
Note: Pro x5 and Pro x20 are not supported — OpenAI uses an embedded Stripe Checkout for those plans, and a shareable link cannot be generated.
